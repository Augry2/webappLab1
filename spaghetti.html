Spaghetti

Here you will find out how to make delicious spaghetti! Follow the simple steps below to create a classic Italian dish that is perfect for dinner, parties, or any time you crave a savory treat. Whether you like it with meatballs, marinara sauce, or simply with olive oil and garlic, this spaghetti recipe is sure to delight your taste buds. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes (if using) and saut√© until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Drain the spaghetti, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water. Add the spaghetti to the skillet with the garlic and toss to combine.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley. If the pasta seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta water, a little at a time, until the desired consistency is reached.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve warm and enjoy!
